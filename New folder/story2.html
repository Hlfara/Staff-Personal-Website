Staff Story

Mr. Syed Ridzuan Bin Syed Mohammad

A UITM alumnus with a Diploma in Graphic Design, he began his career as a graphic designer in a private company in his early twenties. With over 30 years of experience, he advanced his career and became Assistant Director.

Currently serving at Muzium Kota Kayang, he continues to expand his expertise in museum management and administration.

“This museum holds a very wide scope, it is not just about sitting in one place.”

His experience proves that working in this field involves various roles such as data management, geological references, collection documentation, event handling and overall museum administration.

Career Journey

  • 1990 - Started as Graphic Designer
  • 2005 - Joined Muzium Kota Kayang
  • 2015 - Senior Heritage Officer
  • 2022 - Assistant Director
